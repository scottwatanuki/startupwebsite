History of Startups

  • One of the first startups created was Edison General Electric company, or now known as GE.
  • Soon after, in the pre-1900, Silicon Valley was born.
  • The first startup founded in Silicon Valley was Hewlett-Packard, now know as HP.
  • However, the most glorious history of startups started when the Internet became available to the public and many young entrepreneurs started to test out their ideas into the world.

Why Startups?

All of the fascinating apps on your iPhone are created by startups! Not to mention that the devices you use are also created by startups. Startups have the potential to create life-changing products. In a big company, you won't have the flexibility to start something totally new. But in a startup, that's what startups do!
