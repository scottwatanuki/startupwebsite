Why Startup x Exponential Technology?

Startups have been the first to test exponential technologies and release services/products. Startups have the potential to impact millions of people in a very short amount of time, just like Facebook, Google, Amazon, Apple, and other big tech companies have done. With the combination of a startup and exponential technology, anyone can come up with the NEXT iPhone or NEXT Facebook.
